Quick FAQ

What is Normal Block GRUSH?

Normal mode is the classic block-placement mode. Place pieces on the board, complete rows or columns, build combos, and chase a high score.

What is Chaotic Gravity Rush?

Chaotic Gravity Rush adds rotating gravity. After several turns, the board rotates and pieces settle again, creating a more unpredictable puzzle.

How do combos work?

Clear rows or columns across consecutive moves to build a combo streak. Longer streaks can trigger stronger bonus chances and flashier effects.

What are themes?

Themes change the look and feel of the game. Some themes use cozy pastel blocks, while others use brighter arcade-style visuals.

What are rewarded ads used for?

Rewarded ads are optional. They can provide a second-chance retry reward when a run is about to end.

Can I change accessibility options?

Yes. The game includes accessibility options such as Symbol Mode, High Contrast, Reduce Motion, and Haptic Feedback.
